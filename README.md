### Hi there 👋

My name is Paul. I'm a self-taught full stack developer from Romania focusing on .NET and Angular.

Althought I've played with computer programming languages and different frameworks and libraries for a very long time (my first experience with a programming language was at 7 years old teaching myself [Microsoft BASIC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_BASIC) from a book I found in my dad's home library), I never looked at programming as more than just a simple hobby because I thought you had to be a maths genius to become a programmer.

Then the COVID pandemic hit in 2020 and I sat for months at home in front of the computer (because I work as an IT Manager) thinking about what I might do if suddenly I didn't have a job anymore...

During that time, I guess by accident, I found a few YouTube videos from self-taught developers talking about how you can become a software developer at basically any age and... I got really hooked 🤩! I must  have watched dozens of those videos... But I'm grateful to their creators. They got me to trust in myself and in the fact that I can learn programming and become hirable as a full stack developer if I just study consistently and practice by building projects.

So... I quit watching "motivational" videos 😂 and now in 2021 I'm learning Angular, HTML and CSS!

I'm also looking for a job as a developer so if you have something for me, here's my [resume](https://resume.io/r/M9DBn0zOX). Thank you! 😊
